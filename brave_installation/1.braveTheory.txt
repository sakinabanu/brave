BRAVE :

Brave was found by the founder of javascript.
Brave was founded by Brendan Eich and CTO Brian Bondy on 28 May, 2015.
It is used for the security of applications and data.
Brave is a free and open-source web browser developed by Brave Software, Inc. based on the Chromium web browser. 
Brave is a privacy-focused browser, which automatically blocks some advertisements and website trackers in its default settings. 
Brave Software's headquarters are in San Francisco, California.
On 20 January 2016, Brave Software launched the first version of Brave with ad-blocking capabilities and announced plans for an ad platform that uses "browser-side anonymous targeting".
